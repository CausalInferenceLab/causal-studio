Let's recap. Fuzzy RDD is a method that uses eligibility as an instrument to estimate the effect on compliers — the local average treatment effect, or LATE — for cases where the cutoff doesn't fully determine treatment.

We saw that this value can be estimated in the form of the Wald estimator, and that its confidence interval can be obtained through 2SLS.
